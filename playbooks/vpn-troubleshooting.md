## VPN Troubleshooting Playbook

### Symptoms
- VPN fails to connect
- Authentication error
- Connected but no internal access

### Possible Causes
- Password recently changed
- Device time/date mismatch
- Outdated VPN client
- Network restrictions

### Resolution Steps
1. Verify internet access
2. Confirm credentials
3. Sync system time/date
4. Restart VPN and device
5. Reinstall VPN client if needed

### Escalation Criteria
- Multiple users impacted
- Suspected VPN outage
- Issue persists after reinstall
